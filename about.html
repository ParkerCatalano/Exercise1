Life Thus Far...

Since I was just a youngin, I've been drawing and making art with various mediums. I eventually got into School of the Arts in San Francisco where, after they discontinued the guitar program I had originally intended to apply for, I subsequently studied visual art for three years. Although my college career ended up being a Literary one, I am still as fond of visual art as I ever was, and feel that I will be able to incorporate my skills into the programming and development world in a front-end design capacity. Work Experience

I began working at a startup called MOG founded by David Hyman, Co-founder of the track-finding software Gracenote in 2006. It was a summer job and I was doing mostly QA and beta testing for the site's various widgets and extensions, as well as some blog post editing. It was technically a paid internship, and I was very fortunate to be able to experience a workplace like that at my age. It taught me a lot of very valuable lessons about the startup world and the problems that face even a well funded website. This is no industry for the faint-hearted.
